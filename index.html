Career Path
Becoming a CPA

// The Certified Public Accountant credential is among the most respected in all of business. Here's the complete roadmap.

Roadmap

Steps to CPA Licensure

// STEP_01
Earn a Bachelor's in Accounting
4-year degree covering financial accounting, auditing, taxation, business law, and managerial accounting.
// STEP_02
Complete 150 Credit Hours
30 credits beyond a standard degree. Most candidates pursue a Master of Accounting (MAcc) or a 5-year BS/MS program.
// STEP_03
Gain 1–2 Years Under a CPA
Public accounting firms, corporate finance, or government roles all qualify. Requirements vary by state.
// STEP_04
Pass the CPA Exam (4 Sections)
16-hour exam. Score 75+ on each section. All four must be passed within a rolling 30-month window.
// STEP_05
Apply for Your State License
Submit transcripts, work verification, and exam scores to your state board of accountancy.
// STEP_06
Maintain via CPE (40 hrs/year)
Continuing Professional Education keeps your license active and skills current year after year.
